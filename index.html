Sheep House Signature

Elegant, balanced, floral citrus harmony. For guests who want "something special." A deeply aromatic experience where the floral notes of lavender dance perfectly with the lemon-lime sparkle of the cider. This drink is essentially a garden in a glass. It is highly recommended for the daydreamer—someone who enjoys reading a good book in a quiet corner and appreciates delicate, perfumed flavors over heavy alcohol kicks.

Recipe
Lavender Gin2 parts (40ml)
Yuzu Liqueur1 part (20ml)
Sake2 parts (40ml)
Lemon Sour1 part (20ml)
